Hello! I'm Will. This is my permanent cyber space.

I was born before the internet became widely available. As a teenager, I used various blogging platforms to document my life, but found them unreliable and lacking control over my content. Even now, I struggle to fully own and control my content on social media. However, during the Christmas week of 2022, I learned how to build websites and am excited to share my thoughts, experiences, and journey with you through my own personal website.
